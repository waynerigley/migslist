Important Disclaimer and Verification Notice

Research Date: December 29, 2025

Data Currency: Union leadership positions change regularly through elections and appointments. This information was accurate as of the research date but should be verified before use.

Verification Required: Before initiating any outreach campaign, please verify:

  • Current position holders (elections may have occurred)
  • Contact information accuracy
  • Appropriate communication channels
  • Privacy and data protection compliance

Data Source: All information compiled from publicly available sources including union websites, press releases, and news articles.

Completeness: This directory focuses on major provincial and significant local union leadership. Many additional local union presidents exist but are not included in this compilation.
